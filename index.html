FAQS

Our bracelets often use materials like metal, leather, beads, or gemstones.

 

Clean and maintain your bracelet according to its material; use a soft cloth for metal and avoid harsh chemicals.

All of our bracelets can withstand water, but it's safe to remove them before showering or swimming.

Some of our bracelets offer custom sizing, and many can be adjusted for different wrist sizes.

To keep your bracelet in top condition, avoid harsh chemicals and moisture. Use a soft cloth for cleaning, and follow any care instructions, especially for gemstones, to maintain its longevity and appearance.
